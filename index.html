Welcome
to Loyal rescue.

Loyal rescue was founded in 2009 by a group of dedicated people who wanted to make a difference in the world. We are a non-for-profit organization who believe that the world can be a kinder place, where people care about animals and make any effort to work together for the well-being of the pets.

To reach our goal we try our best to take care of the rescued animals and provide them with temporary home and care until we find them well-matched families. Another goal of our organization is to educate responsible pet owners providing them with the great library of resources and tips, so no pet would be abandoned and mistreated.

Loyal rescue doesn’t receive any government funding, it depends on fundraising events, donations and adoption fees to pay for animal food, health care and our operating costs.
